Resume Guide

How to write resume 101

In this lesson you will learn how to properly write a resume. Writing a resume is not as simple as you may think and it's almost essential in terms of getting a job. Remember that your resume is really your first impression and it gives the reader an outlook on you. So, be sure to check your grammar, spelling and punctuation.
